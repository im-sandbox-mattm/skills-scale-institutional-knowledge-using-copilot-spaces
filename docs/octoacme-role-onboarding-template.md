# OctoAcme Role Onboarding Template

## Purpose
This template provides a structured approach for onboarding new team members into specific roles on OctoAcme projects. Use this template to create role-specific onboarding plans and track completion.

## How to Use This Template
1. Copy this template for each new team member
2. Select the appropriate role-specific checklist from the sections below
3. Customize the checklist based on project context and specific needs
4. Track progress and mark items as complete
5. Schedule follow-up meetings to address any gaps or questions

---

## General Onboarding (All Roles)

### Week 1: Orientation
- [ ] Complete company/organization orientation
- [ ] Set up accounts and access to tools
- [ ] Review project management overview document
- [ ] Meet with team lead or manager for role expectations discussion
- [ ] Review project charter and current status
- [ ] Join project communication channels (Slack, Teams, etc.)
- [ ] Attend team standup or status meeting

### Week 2: Deep Dive
- [ ] Review relevant project documentation
- [ ] Meet with key stakeholders in your domain
- [ ] Shadow experienced team member in your role
- [ ] Begin taking on initial tasks with support
- [ ] Set up 1:1s with key collaborators

### Week 3-4: Ramp Up
- [ ] Take ownership of specific work items
- [ ] Participate actively in team ceremonies
- [ ] Provide feedback on onboarding process
- [ ] Identify areas for process improvement
- [ ] Complete role-specific training (if applicable)

## Role-Specific Checklists

### Release Manager Onboarding
- [ ] Review release and deployment guide
- [ ] Understand deployment pipeline and infrastructure
- [ ] Access release management tools and dashboards
- [ ] Review past release retrospectives and lessons learned
- [ ] Meet with QA Lead to understand quality gates
- [ ] Shadow at least one release cycle
- [ ] Review rollback procedures and incident playbooks
- [ ] Establish communication channels with key stakeholders
- [ ] Review compliance and readiness checklists
- [ ] Understand post-deployment monitoring and validation processes

### Business Analyst Onboarding
- [ ] Review product roadmap and current backlog
- [ ] Understand business domains and stakeholder landscape
- [ ] Access requirements management tools
- [ ] Review user story templates and acceptance criteria standards
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager to align on prioritization
- [ ] Shadow requirements gathering sessions
- [ ] Review existing business process documentation
- [ ] Establish communication channels with key business stakeholders
- [ ] Understand approval workflows and decision-making processes
- [ ] Practice documenting requirements with feedback from experienced BA

### Communications Lead Onboarding
- [ ] Review project management overview and communication cadence
- [ ] Access project dashboards and reporting tools
- [ ] Review past status reports and stakeholder updates
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to understand current priorities
- [ ] Identify all stakeholder groups and their information needs
- [ ] Review communication templates and standards
- [ ] Establish distribution lists and communication channels
- [ ] Create communication calendar and schedule
- [ ] Understand escalation paths for communication issues
- [ ] Review brand/messaging guidelines (if applicable)

### QA Lead Onboarding
- [ ] Review test strategy and quality standards
- [ ] Access test management and automation tools
- [ ] Review current test coverage and quality metrics
- [ ] Meet with Tech Lead to understand architecture and testability
- [ ] Review quality gates and definition of done
- [ ] Shadow test planning and execution sessions
- [ ] Review defect tracking process and tools
- [ ] Establish communication with development team
- [ ] Understand regression testing and automation strategy
- [ ] Review performance and security testing requirements

### Change Coordinator Onboarding
- [ ] Review change management policy and procedures
- [ ] Access change management tools and systems
- [ ] Review change request templates and approval workflows
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to understand current change pipeline
- [ ] Review past change advisory board meetings
- [ ] Understand escalation paths and approval authorities
- [ ] Review change categorization and risk assessment criteria
- [ ] Establish communication with key approvers and stakeholders
- [ ] Understand emergency change procedures
- [ ] Review post-implementation review process

### Developer Onboarding
- [ ] Set up development environment
- [ ] Review codebase architecture and documentation
- [ ] Access version control and CI/CD systems
- [ ] Review coding standards and contribution guidelines
- [ ] Complete first code review
- [ ] Submit first pull request
- [ ] Participate in design discussions
- [ ] Understand testing and quality requirements
- [ ] Review security best practices
- [ ] Meet with QA Lead to understand test strategy

### Product Manager Onboarding
- [ ] Review product vision and strategy documents
- [ ] Access product analytics and metrics dashboards
- [ ] Review customer feedback and research data
- [ ] Meet with stakeholders to understand priorities
- [ ] Review competitive landscape and market analysis
- [ ] Understand roadmap planning process
- [ ] Shadow user research or customer interviews
- [ ] Review pricing and business model (if applicable)
- [ ] Establish cadence with engineering and design teams
- [ ] Review product launch and go-to-market processes

### Project Manager Onboarding
- [ ] Review project charter and scope
- [ ] Access project management tools (Jira, Asana, etc.)
- [ ] Review current project plan and timeline
- [ ] Meet with all key stakeholders
- [ ] Review risk register and mitigation strategies
- [ ] Understand resource allocation and constraints
- [ ] Review meeting cadence and facilitation expectations
- [ ] Establish reporting and status update processes
- [ ] Review budget and financial tracking (if applicable)
- [ ] Understand escalation paths and decision-making authority

## 30-60-90 Day Goals

### 30 Days
- Complete all onboarding checklist items
- Develop working relationships with key collaborators
- Understand current project status and priorities
- Begin contributing to role responsibilities with guidance
- Identify immediate questions or knowledge gaps

### 60 Days
- Take full ownership of core role responsibilities
- Proactively contribute to team discussions and decisions
- Establish regular communication patterns with stakeholders
- Provide feedback on processes and suggest improvements
- Demonstrate proficiency in role-specific tools and systems

### 90 Days
- Operate independently in role with minimal oversight
- Mentor or support other team members as appropriate
- Drive initiatives or improvements within area of responsibility
- Build strong stakeholder relationships and trust
- Contribute to team knowledge base and documentation
